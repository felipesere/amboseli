Getting to and from St Alban

Transport in St Albans

  • Trains: St Albans City train station- main train station with direct trains from Luton Airport & trains into London. Use the The Trainline website to plan your journey.
  • Buses: There are a number of buses running through the city. Use the Arriva bus website to plan your journey.
  • Taxis: We have been recommended Corker Taxi by St Michael's Manor. There is a taxi rank at St Albans City train station, and there are many taxi companies in St Albans.

Airport Transport

If you prefer to organise a taxi collection see above for recommendations. To find relevant trains please use The Trainline website.

  • London Luton Airport - direct trains from Luton Airport Parkway to St Albans (approx 15mins)
  • London Gatwick Airport - direct or 1 stop trains to St Albans (approx 1hr).
  • London Heathrow Airport - 1 stop trains to St Albans (approx 1hr 20 mins). There is also a bus , check the arriva website (approx 1hr 25 mins)
  • London Stansted Airport - 2 stops trains to St Albans (approx 1hr 40 mins)
