Trains

St Albans City train station is the main train station with direct trains from Luton Airport & trains into London. Use the The Trainline website to plan your journey.

Buses üöå

There are a number of buses running through the city. Use the Arriva bus website to plan your journey.

Taxis

We have been recommended Corker Taxi by St Michael's Manor. There is a taxi rank at St Albans City train station, and there are many taxi companies in St Albans. Very practically, the train station is within sight of the registry office. This should make it easy, should you want to get a taxi from the registry office to St Michael's Manor.

Airport Transport

There is no airport in St Albans, but there are plenty of choices around London!

Luton Airport

Probably the closes one to get to. There are regular buses that take you from the terminal to the nearby train station. Trains from Luton Airport Parkway to St Albans take approx 15mins, without having to change anywhere.

London Luton Airport

Gatwick Airport

Gatwick is on the other side of London, in the south. But it has the advantage that there are direct trains from Gatwick up to St. Albans. They take about 1hr and you get some inivial views of London, if you travel during the day.

London Gatwick Airport

Heathrow Airport

Londons main airport. It should be easy to get flights here. Due to its location out West, its a bit more work to get to St Albans. You can take the Elizabeth line (its brand new!) into London Farringdon station and take a direct train St Albans from there. Alternatively, we dound out there is also a bus that goes from Heathrow to the center of St. Albans. It takes about 1h 30mins. More details on Arriva.

London Heathrow Airport

Stansted Airport

Located out east, the transport situtation is similar to London Heathrow airport. Trains from there to St Albans require about 1hr 40mins and involve multiple changes.

London Stansted Airport

If you prefer to organise a taxi collection see above for recommendations. To find relevant trains we suggest using the Trainline website.
